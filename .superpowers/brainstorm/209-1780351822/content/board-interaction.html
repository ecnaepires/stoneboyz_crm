The key decision: how does a job land in the right column? This shapes the whole build.

A

Manual drag

You drag a card from one column to the next when you decide it's there. The stage is just "whatever column you dropped it in." Simple, full control. Risk: the board can drift from what's actually scheduled (you forget to drag).

B

Auto-derived from appointments

Cards move themselves. When you finish a Template appointment, the job jumps to Template→next stage automatically. Board is mostly read-only and always matches reality. Risk: less manual control; needs an appointment to exist for each stage.

C

Hybrid — auto-suggest, manual override

Stage auto-advances from finished appointments, but you can also drag a card to override. Best of both, most work to build. Usually a later upgrade once A or B proves out.
